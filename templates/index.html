Shakespearean MadLib

The Concept

For those unfamiliar with the game MadLibs, it is a game in which a fun story is presented with certain key words missing and the player's job is to fill in the blanks without any context as to the story itself, making for a very humorous time. This Shakespearean MadLib is much the same, only the stories are scenes taken from your choice of Shakespeare's plays.
